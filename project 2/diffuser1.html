Gyro Diffuser
Overview
This diffuser can be added with three different essential oils. In addition, as a switch and anti-toppling, the shape of the gyro can also allow the user to interact with the diffuser to express stress and relaxation.

Research

In Taiwan, aromatherapy machines are the most common product. People like to use them to express stress and relaxation, however, they are not suitable because Taiwan is very humid. Therefore, the diffuser is the most suitable, but the current product can only drip one kind of essential oil.

Concept

I divide a day into three moments: refreshing in the morning, relaxing in the afternoon, and helping sleep at night, so that users can enjoy three different essential oils in a day. Most importantly, the appearance of the gyro is anti-dumping.

mechanism
Using Bernoulli's principle to atomize the essential oil so that users do not need to add water to it. The advantage is that the essential oil is relatively pure.
There are three touch points on the top, each of which can trigger a nozzle and one side of LED, so that the user can easily know which nozzle is working now.
When the user presses it, the steel ball will fall into electrical contact and the diffuser will lean on that side by weight, and the air pump will be started.
Use wireless charging so that the user can use it anywhere without having to carry wire behind the product.
