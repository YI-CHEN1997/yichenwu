Button Cover
Overview
Nowadays, there are few button styles to choose from and the old buttons must be removed. It is troublesome to use. Therefore, I design a button cover which can be put on directly. Moreover, due to the characteristics of ceramics, essential oils and perfume can be dripped on it, which can prevent skin allergy.
